Ballet Details in LNB Dance Academy



A ballet, a work, consists of the choreography and music for a ballet production.

Ballets are choreographed and performed by trained ballet dancers.

Traditional classical ballets are usually performed with classical music accompaniment and use elaborate

costumes and staging, whereas modern ballets, such as the neoclassical works of American choreographer George Balanchine,
often are performed in simple costumes (e.g., leotards and tights) and without the use of elaborate sets or scenery.
